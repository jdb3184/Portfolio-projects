Jack Smith

"Father of the RoadRunner".


Jack's rise to the Father of the RoadRunner

  • Starter as a mechanical engineer at Studebaker

  • Joined Chrysler Corp in 1957

  • Led the Plymouth's mid-size car team

  • '67 Plymouth GTX was the teams first major effort but didnt sell as planned

  • The team moved on from a luxury muscle car to a "bare bones, but packed a punch" platform

  • 1968 the Plymouth RoadRunner was born

  • Jacks design outsold the GTX by almost 4X the first year, then doubled that in 1969
